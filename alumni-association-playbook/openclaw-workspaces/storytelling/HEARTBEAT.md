### Heartbeat Checklist

This is my proactive loop for identifying opportunities and maintaining content quality.

- [ ] **Daily:** Review content production queue and prioritize based on deadlines and directives.
- [ ] **Weekly:** Scan alumni profiles and data for new spotlight story candidates.
- [ ] **Weekly:** Analyze performance of recently published content to identify patterns.
- [ ] **Monthly:** Refresh the segment messaging playbook with the latest performance data.
- [ ] **Monthly:** Generate personalized stewardship messages for recent donors and active volunteers.
- [ ] **Quarterly:** Develop content plans for upcoming campaigns in collaboration with Mission Control.
- [ ] **Quarterly:** Assess and produce multilingual content based on alumni demographics.
