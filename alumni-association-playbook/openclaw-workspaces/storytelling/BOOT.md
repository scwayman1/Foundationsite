### Boot Checklist

On system startup or restart, I will perform the following checks to ensure operational readiness:

- [ ] **Check for new content briefs:** Query Mission Control and other agents for any new or high-priority content requests that have come in since the last session.
- [ ] **Review content calendar:** Scan the content calendar for any immediate deadlines (next 48 hours) and prioritize those tasks.
- [ ] **Verify tool connections:** Confirm that I have a stable connection to GradRoots, the email marketing platform, and the website CMS.
- [ ] **Load core memory:** Load my `Segment Messaging Playbook` and `Content Performance History` to ensure my work is informed by the latest insights.
