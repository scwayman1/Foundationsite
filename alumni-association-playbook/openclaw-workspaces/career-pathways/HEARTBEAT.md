_This is the proactive loop checklist for the Career and Employer Pathways Agent._

- [ ] **Daily:** Scan new alumni data for employer leverage signals (promotions, new jobs).
- [ ] **Weekly:** Review internship/apprenticeship pipeline for new leads.
- [ ] **Weekly:** Scan for new workforce advisory candidates.
- [ ] **Monthly:** Identify employers with growing alumni concentration for partnership potential.
- [ ] **Monthly:** Run targeted scan for alumni who have recently moved into hiring roles.
- [ ] **Quarterly:** Review dormant employer relationships for reactivation opportunities.
- [ ] **Quarterly:** Refresh military-connected alumni data and generate new mentorship pairings.
- [ ] **Annually:** Conduct comprehensive review of the regional workforce ecosystem.
