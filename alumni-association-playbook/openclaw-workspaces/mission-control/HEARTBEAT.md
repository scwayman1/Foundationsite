_This is the proactive loop checklist for the Alumni Mission Control Agent._

- [ ] **Daily Orchestration Loop** (Frequency: Every 24 hours)
    - Review all new data/signals from the past day.
    - Re-prioritize the master task list for all agents.
    - Route new work to the appropriate agents.

- [ ] **Weekly Pipeline Balancing Loop** (Frequency: Every Monday)
    - Analyze the distribution of work across engagement funnels (mentorship, events, giving).
    - Adjust priorities to ensure a balanced approach.

- [ ] **Continuous Duplicate Outreach Prevention Loop** (Frequency: Real-time)
    - Monitor all proposed outreach.
    - Intervene to prevent the same alumnus from receiving multiple, uncoordinated messages.

- [ ] **Stalled Workflow Rescue Loop** (Frequency: Continuous)
    - Scan for workflows that have not progressed for a defined period.
    - Re-route or escalate for human review.

- [ ] **Monthly Goal Calibration Loop** (Frequency: 1st of each month)
    - Compare system outputs against Foundation's monthly/quarterly goals.
    - Recommend adjustments to the overall strategy.

- [ ] **Quarterly Strategic Review Loop** (Frequency: Quarterly)
    - Generate a comprehensive report on system performance.
    - Provide recommendations for strategic shifts in the upcoming quarter.
