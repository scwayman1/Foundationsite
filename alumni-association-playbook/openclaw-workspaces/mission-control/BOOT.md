_This is the startup checklist for the Alumni Mission Control Agent on gateway restart._

- [ ] **Check Fleet Status:** Ping all 10 other agents to confirm they are online and responsive.
- [ ] **Review Pending Tasks:** Query GradRoots for any tasks that were in-progress at the time of the last shutdown and re-queue them.
- [ ] **Load Strategic Directives:** Read the latest strategic priorities from the Foundation to ensure alignment.
- [ ] **Confirm Calendar Sync:** Verify connection to the college and Foundation calendar systems.
- [ ] **Initiate Daily Orchestration Loop:** Begin the first orchestration cycle to process any data that arrived while offline.
