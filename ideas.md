## Selected Approach: Modern Corporate Minimalism with Academic Prestige

I will proceed with the **Modern Corporate Minimalism with Academic Prestige** approach. This style best aligns with the Foundation's goals of building trust with donors (Navy Blue), showcasing growth and innovation (Teal), and highlighting excellence (Gold). It strikes the right balance between professional reliability and modern appeal, which is crucial for a fundraising and strategic planning platform. The asymmetric layouts and subtle geometric patterns will provide the "modern spruce up" requested without sacrificing the gravitas required for a financial foundation.

**Design Philosophy**:
- **Colors**: Navy Blue (#0f172a), Teal (#0d9488), Gold (#f59e0b), White (#ffffff), Light Gray (#f8fafc)
- **Typography**: Montserrat (Headings), Open Sans (Body)
- **Visuals**: Clean cards with soft shadows, subtle wave patterns, high-quality imagery.
